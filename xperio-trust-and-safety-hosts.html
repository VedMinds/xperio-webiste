Xperio Trust & Safety Policy for Hosts

Effective Date: 01/07/2024

1. Host Safety Guidelines

To enhance your safety as a host and ensure a positive experience, Xperio provides the following guidelines:

  • Secure Communication: Utilize Xperio’s in-app messaging system to communicate with guests, ensuring that your personal contact information remains private.
  • Guest Screening: Review guest profiles and their reviews before accepting bookings. Use the Xperio platform to ask pre-booking questions if necessary.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Be knowledgeable about emergency procedures and have a plan in place for handling unexpected situations. Keep emergency contacts handy.

2. Secure Booking and Payment System

  • Protected Transactions: All payments are processed securely through Xperio to protect your financial information and ensure you receive payments timely.
  • Payment Policy: Understand and utilize Xperio’s payment policy, which is designed to ensure that you are compensated for your services without delay after the experience is completed.

3. User Verification and Reviews

  • Traveler Verification: Benefit from Xperio's verification processes for travelers to enhance trust and safety for both parties.
  • Host Reviews: After hosting guests, encourage them to leave reviews. Positive reviews build your reputation and help attract more guests.

4. Customer Support and Incident Response

  • 24/7 Support: Access to Xperio’s customer support team is available at any time to assist with any concerns or emergencies you might encounter.
  • Incident Reporting: Use the platform to report any issues you face with guests. Xperio’s team will take necessary actions to resolve conflicts and ensure your safety.

5. Health and Safety Standards

  • Hosting Guidelines: Follow Xperio’s health and safety guidelines designed to protect both you and your guests, especially in light of public health concerns such as COVID-19.

6. Insurance and Protection

  • Host Insurance: Xperio provides insurance options to protect hosts against liabilities and damages, ensuring peace of mind when opening your home or service to guests.
  • Host Guarantee: Covers property damage caused by guests to the host's property, excluding personal liability and theft.
  • Host Protection Insurance: Provides liability coverage for hosts, covering claims made by guests or third parties during their stay.
  • Experiences Liability Insurance: Offers coverage in the event a host is found legally responsible for a guest's injury or property damage during an Xperio Experience.

7. Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Legal Support: Stay informed about local laws and regulations that affect hosting activities. Xperio provides resources and support to help you comply with legal requirements.

8. Feedback and Continuous Improvement

  • Feedback Mechanism: We value your feedback and encourage you to share your hosting experiences to help us improve our services and policies.

9. Updates to Policy

  • Policy Updates: This Trust & Safety policy is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect the latest security measures, technological advancements, and host feedback.

10. Contact Us

For additional inquiries or immediate support, please contact our dedicated host support team at support@xperio.travel.
