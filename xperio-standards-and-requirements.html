Xperio Host Standards and Requirements

Welcome to Xperio! To create authentic, immersive, and sustainable experiences, hosts must adhere to the following standards before listing their offerings on the platform. These guidelines ensure high-quality, safe, and meaningful experiences that align with Xperio’s mission.

1. Core Experience Guidelines

Authenticity:

  • Experiences must reflect local culture, hosted by locals or those with deep ties to the community.
  • Activities should avoid stereotypes and offer genuine representation of local traditions.

Sustainability:

  • Minimize environmental impact by reducing waste, conserving water, and promoting eco-friendly practices.
  • Support local economies by hiring locals and sourcing materials locally.

Cultural Immersion:

  • Offer deep cultural engagement with meaningful interactions.
  • Educate travelers on local customs and respectful behavior.

Quality and Safety:

  • Ensure compliance with local safety regulations.
  • Provide necessary safety equipment and maintain a clean, safe environment.

Personalization & Accessibility:

  • Adapt experiences to guests’ preferences and feedback.
  • Make experiences inclusive and accessible wherever possible.

Community Impact:

  • Create experiences with a positive impact on the local community (e.g., supporting conservation or educational initiatives).
  • Gather and respond to community feedback to maintain benefits for stakeholders.

2. Activities with Special Requirements

Safety & Compliance:

  • Obtain permits and licenses for activities if required.
  • Conduct risk assessments and provide safety briefings to participants.

Environmental and Cultural Responsibility:

  • Practice leave-no-trace principles in natural areas.
  • Respect cultural traditions and secure community consent for culturally sensitive activities.

Emergency Preparedness:

  • Hosts must have emergency plans and basic first-aid knowledge.
  • Provide local emergency contact information to guests.

Health, Age, and Skill Requirements:

  • Clearly communicate health and age restrictions.
  • Provide appropriate training or supervision for activities requiring specific skills.

3. Host and Experience Management

Experience Listing Requirements:

  • Use clear descriptions, high-quality images, and accurate pricing.
  • Provide a complete host profile with contact details for guest inquiries.

Availability and Scheduling:

  • Maintain an up-to-date availability calendar to prevent booking conflicts.
  • Offer flexible scheduling options when possible.

Food Safety Guidelines (If Applicable):

  • Follow food handling protocols to prevent contamination.
  • Communicate allergen information and accommodate dietary restrictions.
  • Frequent cancellations may result in penalties or suspension from the platform.

4. Legal, Tax, and Insurance Compliance

Local Regulations:

  • Hosts must comply with local business laws, taxes, and zoning rules.

Business Ethics:

  • Maintain transparency and honesty in all communication and pricing.
  • Adhere to non-discrimination policies and ensure guest privacy.
  • Obtain necessary insurance for activities involving risks (e.g., adventure sports).

Conclusion

By adhering to these standards, hosts contribute to Xperio’s mission of providing authentic, sustainable, and impactful travel experiences. We are excited to collaborate with you in offering unique experiences that create lasting memories for travelers and empower local communities.

Thank you for being a part of Xperio!
