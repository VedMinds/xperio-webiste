Xperio Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/07/2024

Welcome to Xperio! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our applications. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Xperio Mobile Application or Web Application and provides you with information about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Identification Information: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, aadhar number, and payment information, when you register for an account, book an experience, or contact our support team.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include details such as your device's IP address, browser type, browser version, pages visited, visit time and date, time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

Location Information: We use GPS technology to determine your current location. Some location-enabled services require your personal data to function.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Managing your account and bookings
  • Processing transactions
  • Providing customer support and updates about your bookings
  • Enhancing our applications based on feedback and usage patterns
  • Conducting research and analytics
  • Providing personalized experiences and advertising

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers performing services on our behalf (e.g., payment processing, data analysis, customer service)
  • Business partners involved in your booked experiences
  • Affiliates in our corporate group
  • Law enforcement or government agencies if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities

4. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights under data protection laws regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent at any time when Xperio relied on your consent to process your personal information.

5. Data Security

We use technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or storage can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee complete protection from unauthorized access or cybercriminals.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, with the "Effective Date" updated at the top of this Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at support@xperio.travel.
